Conditions can change between when you book your travel and when you leave. Check the Travel advice page for your destination when planning your trip and shortly before you leave.
Tips to help you prepare for travel

Buy the best travel health insurance you can afford before you leave. Your insurance may be affected if you book travel when we advise against travelling to a destination.
